Validate eMail Address Format

word(.word)@subdomain(.subdomain)
A word is either an atom or a quoted string. An atom is a sequence of following printable ASCII characters:
!"#$%&'*+-/0123456789=?
ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ
^_`
abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz
{|}~.
Space or any of following characters are excepted: ()<>@,;:\".[]
